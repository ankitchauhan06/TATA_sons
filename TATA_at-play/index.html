Mountaineering

Exactly 5 months after joining Tata Steel, on May 23, 1984, Bachendri Pal stood on the summit of Mount Everest, unfurling the Indian tricolour and the Tata flags.

Marathons

As title sponsor or technology partner, TCS has been associated with the city marathons of Mumbai, New York, Boston, London, Singapore and Amsterdam.

The 2018 London Marathon
raised £63.7 million (approx. INR 6
billion) for charity, setting a new
world record for a single-day
charity fundraising event.

On January 15, 2023, a record 55,000+ people in Mumbai woke
up ready to run the Tata Mumbai Marathon held after a 2-year gap.

TCS' marathon app enhanced the experience with features such as
live location, Practice 5k, and
many more.

15 years since its inception, the Tata Mumbai Marathon is the biggest philanthropic sporting
event in India, having raised almost Rs. 2 billion in funds that have benefitted around 550 NGOs.
